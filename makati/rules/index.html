Rules and Regulations

These rules are made to maintain order in the chat room. Everyone is expected to obey these rules and no one is to be above them. Anyone who fails to follow (or deliberately disobeys) is subject to appropriate disciplinary action.

Please respect the Operators as they are just enforcing the channel rules to maintain peace and order in the channel. All operators reserve the right to Ban/Kick anyone without notification and/or reason. This service is a privilege, not a right. Please respect that at all times.

The rules are as follows:

  1. Follow staff requests or warnings.
  2. No Flooding. You are not to exceed 5 lines in 4 seconds.
  3. No Repeating of text. A maximum of 2repeats is allowed.
  4. Avoid provanity, vulgar language/comments/remarks at all times.
  5. No Channel/Website/Server Invites or Advertisement.
  6. Any Sexual Attitudes or behavior is NOT allowed in this channel.
  7. Do not use Excessive Uppercase Letters (CAPs) since it is       considered "shouting/yelling".
  8. We do not tolerate any Offensive Nickname, Ident or Hosts
  9. Excessive clones are not allowed.
 10. Auto Greets are not allowed.
 11. No Op/Voice Begging.
 12. Be civil, polite and follow common net etiquette.
 13. Do not abuse or harass other users in any way.
 14. Chat in Tagalog or English. Other dialect can be permitted by an OP.
 15. You must have fun and ENJOY!
 

Interested to help? Please read application process.
